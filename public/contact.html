Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in UX/UI design for web and mobile applications, including e-commerce sites, SaaS platforms, mobile apps, and brand identity projects. I work with startups, agencies, and established companies across various industries.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple website redesign might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive mobile app could take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I've worked with clients from around the world. I'm flexible with time zones and can accommodate different communication preferences. I'm also fluent in English and Spanish.

What's included in your design process?

My process includes user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and user testing. I provide regular updates and iterations throughout the project, ensuring you're involved in every step of the design process.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! I offer ongoing support packages for maintenance, updates, and improvements. I believe in building long-term relationships with clients and ensuring their digital products continue to perform well.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

I include multiple rounds of revisions in my project scope. I encourage open communication and feedback throughout the process to ensure the final design meets your expectations and business goals.
