Overview

Project Overview

Keyfield is a real estate web application designed to streamline the home-buying process by connecting users directly with professionals, providing detailed property information, and enabling a more personalized experience. Users can view current listings, explore the history of a property, and access specific details such as square footage, renovations, and neighborhood insights.

Problem & Solution

🚨 Problems

🏠 For first-time buyers, the process is often confusing and emotional.

📚 Existing resources, such as blogs or agencies, can be overwhelming or too generic.

⏰ Many buyers waste time viewing properties outside their budget or needs.

💡 Solution

A responsive web app designed for new property buyers.

Offers clear guidance, expert insights, and personalized property information. Helps users save time and make confident, informed decisions.

Key Features

🏠 Current property listings with detailed information

📊 Property history and renovation details

🔍 Advanced search and filtering capabilities

👥 Direct connection with real estate professionals

📝 Qualitative Research

I conducted interviews with first-time home buyers to understand:

Home buying challenges and frustrations Information needs and preferences

🔍 Some Questions I asked:

What challenges did you face when buying your first home?
What information do you wish you had when house hunting?
How do you currently research properties and neighborhoods?
What would make the home buying process easier for you?

💡 Insights:

🏠

First-time buyers need guidance and reassurance throughout the process

📊

Detailed property information helps buyers make informed decisions

👥

Direct access to professionals reduces anxiety and confusion

📊 Quantitative Research

I conducted surveys with potential home buyers to understand market trends and user preferences in the real estate space.

Market trends and user preferences Statistical data and user behavior

📊 Survey Data Collected:

How long do first-time buyers typically spend researching before making a decision?
What percentage of buyers feel overwhelmed by the home buying process?
What information do buyers prioritize when evaluating properties?
How important is access to detailed property history and neighborhood insights?

💡 Key Findings:

📊

85% of first-time buyers feel overwhelmed by the home buying process

⏰

72% spend 3+ months researching before making a decision

🏠

90% want detailed property history and neighborhood insights

Security Features

Search Functionality

The search feature was well-received but needed more advanced filtering options

📱

Mobile Experience

Mobile interface was intuitive but needed better touch targets for key actions

🔄

Key Organization

Users wanted more flexible categorization and tagging systems for their keys

💾

Backup & Sync

Cross-platform synchronization was essential but needed clearer setup instructions

🎯 User-Centered Design

Designing for first-time buyers taught me the importance of clarity and simplicity in creating an efficient and easy-to-understand experience.

🧪 Usability Testing Impact

I learned the value of visual clarity. Correct spacing, hierarchy, and typography help users easily navigate through screens.

🔄 Feedback & Iteration

Consistent buttons and well-placed images made the UI more intuitive and visually engaging.
